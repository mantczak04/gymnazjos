# Nutrition Meals Route

This route renders meal template management.

- Delegate behavior to `MealLibraryPage`.
- Meal templates are one-serving reusable templates built only from Product Library products.
- Do not add meal serving multipliers, meal notes, recipe instructions, or manual ingredient ordering in the MVP.
- Deactivate historical meal templates instead of hard deleting them.
- Keep product creation inside meal-template flows routed through nutrition feature components and queries.
